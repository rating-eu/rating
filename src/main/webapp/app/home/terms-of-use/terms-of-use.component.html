RATING is a risk assessment tool, which supports organizations to assess collective evidence-based risk profiles, or organizational risk profiles. It includes both qualitative and quantitative methodologies based on risk definition from ISO31000 and ISO 31010 risk management and risk assessment techniques standards to protect the assets from cyber-threat attacks. It is able to identify major cybersecurity risks and support decisions related to cyber-security investments on hard and soft mitigation solutions.
